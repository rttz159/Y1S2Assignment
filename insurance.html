Things to note about your insurance:
  1. This comprehensive policy does not cover losses due to natural disasters such as floods and landslides. For that, you can insure yourself with Special Peril cover. Nonetheless, you are entitled to claim RM1,000 allowance for any flood damage subject to terms & conditions.
  2. Windscreen claims do not affect your No Claim Discount (NCD) if you have separate/additional windscreen coverage.
  3. Each additional driver cost of RM10 will be waived by LUX-G Insurance Program All Driver coverage.
  4. All company-registered vehicles must have all their drivers insured.
  5. The above coverage of Key Cover, Flood Allowance & Policyholder Personal Accident claims will not affect your No Claim Discount (NCD).
  6. If you have more than one vehicle registered under the same owner, your NCD is transferrable between those vehicles.
